My dream

My dream is to be able to cure and help treat disease and disability patients. I also want to be able to find super effective cures for the worst diseases and disabillities. My sister has Sickle Cell is a red blood cell disorder in which there aren't enough healthy red blood cells to carry oxygen throughout your body. My dad has an ulcer which gives pain that often comes several hours after a meal when the stomach is empty. The pain is often worse at night and early morning. I hope to one day help them. I want to be able to help people with disabilities because they have limits to what they can do. I hope to one day cure these diseases so that nobody has to go their whole life with them. I want people to be able to live normal lives without worrying that they can’t do something. I hope to one day help them all. They don’t have control over what happens in their life.
